About the brand

KyKy Clothing creates clean typography focused designs with subtle graphic accents. The style favors strong statements and minimal layouts that print well on many apparel colors. Every asset on this page was made by the brand owner.

Workflow

  • Layout in Canva or Adobe tools
  • Vector tweaks in Illustrator and raster finishing in Photoshop as needed
  • Print ready PNG size 4500 by 5400 pixels when required
